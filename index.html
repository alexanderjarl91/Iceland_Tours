About Us

See Iceland Tours was founded in 2018 by a couple of adventurous friends that wanted to share their unique experiences and love for Iceland with some like-minded people.

Between them, John and Jón have almost three decades experience as tour-guides, both in and outside of Iceland. Their mantra is that life is there to be enjoyed and they are willing to guarantee that with See Iceland Tours you’ll have the experience of a lifetime.

Our Tours

Into the Glacier

Being surrounded by the magnificent blue walls of a glacier tunnel is completely unwordly. And you’ll have lots of amazing pictures for your Instagram.

Drive Cross Country

Unique tour driving from corner to corner across the highlands. Starting in the South-West, the tour takes you accross lunar landscapes up to the North-East.

Bird's Eye View

What is even more magnificent than seeing the amazing Icelandic landscape on the ground? Seeing it from above. A helicopter tour gives you the bird’s eye view.
